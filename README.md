# NaariCares-PCOD-PCOS-AI-HEALTHCARE
 NaariCares, an AI powered healthcare and awareness platform designed to support women dealing with PCOS/PCOD it provides guidance, early risk awareness, lifestyle assistance, medication reminders and ethical AI based decision support. The application focuses on empowerment, prevention, accessibility rather than replacing professional medical care.

## Introduction
Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) and Polycystic Ovary Disease (PCOD) are among the most
common hormonal disorders affecting women, particularly in India. Despite high prevalence, lack of awareness, delayed diagnosis, social stigma and, misinformation continue to worsen health outcomes.
NaariCares is an AI‑powered healthcare and awareness platform designed to support women dealing with PCOS/PCOD by providing reliable guidance, early‑risk awareness, lifestyle assistance, medication reminders and ethical AI‑based decision support. The application focuses on empowerment, prevention and accessibility rather than replacing professional medical care.

## Problem Statement
Women suffering from PCOS/PCOD face multiple challenges:
 - Lack of early awareness and symptom understanding
 - Difficulty maintaining diet and lifestyle consistency
 - Missed medications due to irregular schedules
 - Limited access to gynecologists, especially while traveling
 - Confusion caused by misinformation from online sources
 - Poor integration between symptoms, diet and healthcare access

## Objectives of SheCares
 - To assist users in managing diet, lifestyle, and medication
 - To create awareness about PCOS/PCOD symptoms and risks
 - To provide ethical AI‑based guidance without medical diagnosis
 - To support women during travel or unfamiliar environments
 - To ensure data privacy, inclusivity and accessibility
 - To encourage timely professional medical consultation 

## Proposed Solution
NaariCares is a mobile and web‑based AI healthcare assistant that integrates symptom analysis, medication reminders, diet planning and location‑based healthcare support under one platform.
The app works as a companion, not a replacement for doctors helping users make informed decisions while prioritizing safety and ethics.

## Key Features
#1 AI‑Based Symptom Awareness Assistant:
 - Users can describe symptoms such as irregular periods, acne, weight fluctuation,
   fatigue, or mood changes.
 - The AI analyzes patterns and provides awareness‑level insights
 - Clear disclaimers are shown: "This is not a diagnosis. Please consult a gynecologist."
   
#2 Medication Reminder System: 
 - Users can upload prescriptions or manually enter medication details
 - Personalized reminders are generated based on dosage and schedule
 - Alerts help maintain consistency in treatment
 - No medication recommendations are generated by AI

#3 AI‑Driven Diet & Lifestyle Guidance
 - The user can report current symptoms or discomfort
 - AI suggests PCOS/PCOD‑friendly dietary guidance
 - The assistant informs:
    1. What foods to avoid temporarily
    2. What foods help reduce inflammation or insulin resistance
    3. Lifestyle suggestions such as hydration, sleep, and light exercise
 - Recommendations are culturally tailored to Indian dietary habits
   
#4 Location‑Based Health Assistance (Permission‑Based)
   a) Healthy Restaurant Locator
 - When the user is out of town, the app can access location only with permission
 - The AI identifies nearby restaurants offering:
    1. Low‑GI meals
    2. Home‑style or balanced food options
    3. PCOS/PCOD‑friendly diets
 - This helps users avoid junk or hormone‑triggering foods while traveling
  b) Nearby Gynecologist & Healthcare Locator
 - On request, the app locates nearby:
    1. Gynecologists
    2. Women’s health clinics
    3. Hospitals
 - No ranking or medical endorsement is done
 - The feature improves access to professional care in unfamiliar areas

## Data Privacy, Security & Ethics
  NaariCares follows a privacy‑first and ethical AI approach:
   - All data is encrypted at rest and in transit
   - No unnecessary personal data is collected
   - Location access is optional and temporary
   - No medical diagnosis or prescription is provided
   - User consent is mandatory for sensitive features
   - Compliance with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Ac

## Conclusion
NaariCares is a holistic, ethical, and impact‑driven AI healthcare application designed to support women suffering from PCOS/PCOD.
